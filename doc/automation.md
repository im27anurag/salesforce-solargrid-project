# Automation Documentation

## Overview

Automation was implemented using Salesforce Flow and platform automation features to reduce manual effort and improve operational efficiency.

---

# Automations Implemented

## Lead Assignment Automation

### Purpose
Automatically assign leads based on business rules and customer requirements.

### Business Benefit
- Faster lead routing
- Reduced manual assignment effort
- Improved response time

---

## Follow-Up Task Automation

### Purpose
Automatically create follow-up tasks after lead or opportunity creation.

### Business Benefit
- Improved sales follow-up tracking
- Better customer engagement
- Reduced missed follow-ups

---

## Case Assignment Automation

### Purpose
Automatically route support cases to appropriate support queues.

### Queues Used
- Technical Support
- Escalation Queue
- Billing Support

### Business Benefit
- Faster case routing
- Better support management
- Reduced manual operations

---

## Email-to-Case Automation

### Purpose
Automatically convert incoming customer emails into support cases.

### Business Benefit
- Automated support intake
- Centralized issue management
- Improved support tracking

---

## Auto-Response Automation

### Purpose
Automatically send acknowledgement emails to customers after case creation.

### Business Benefit
- Improved customer communication
- Faster acknowledgement handling
- Enhanced support experience

---

# Automation Tools Used

- Salesforce Flow
- Assignment Rules
- Auto-Response Rules
- Email-to-Case
- Omni-Channel Concepts

---

# Outcome

The implemented automation reduced repetitive manual tasks, improved response efficiency, and streamlined customer support and sales operations.
